## Packing List

Obviously bring everything you'd normally need as a human on a couple day mountain trip. Some specific call outs:

- Hiking boots / trail runners
- Daypack
- Water bottles / hydration bladder
- Hat
- Layers (warm + rain shell)
  - Definitely at least an outer/rain shell
  - Fleece/warmth layer is nice for colder nights/mornings
- Swimsuit for river dip and hot spring
- Games/Cards
